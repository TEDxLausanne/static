What is your “Idea Worth Spreading”? Make a 3-minute video creatively expressing your story or idea related to the theme of our event “Inter:face Inter:act” and submit it on our website. If we LOVE your video, you will be among a highly select set of individuals invited to speak at TEDxLausanne 2015 in February (date to be defined)!

Submit your video

The content of your talk What should my video be about?

  • The video MUST be linked to the theme for TEDxLausanne 2015, “Inter:face Inter:act”.
  • Make it creative, inspiring, daring, memorable – in short – delight us so we call you in!
  • The quality of the content is more important than the quality of the video itself. We want to see you speaking!
  • We suggest you check out the TEDx Speaker Guidelines and watch this talk by Chris Anderson, curator of TED on what makes a great talk … great.

I have a great idea, but I actually heard, read or saw it somewhere else before

We want YOUR idea, not one that has been borrowed, stolen, or plagiarized from someone or somewhere else.


While we say “yes” to most things, we do have some “NO-NO’s” in the making of this video and in TEDxLausanne talks in general

  • No pseudoscience! Check this blog for more details.
  • No commercial selling
  • No use of profanities, prejudices, slurs of any kind
  • No polarizing views on any subject
  • No propaganda of any kind in making this video – political, religious, cultural or other

The format of your video:


How long does my video need to be?

Give us an impression of you and your idea in three minutes or less.


What format does my video need to be?

It doesn’t matter as long as you can upload it to youtube or vimeo. You can do it privately and simply submit the link.


What language?

The event will be held in English so please submit a video in English.


What style should my video be?

We are giving you creative license to use your own style, however, remember whatever your style is, it should tell a compelling story of your idea worth spreading related to “Inter:face Inter:act”.


Can I use music/photos/animation in my video?

Sure, if it helps, use it! BUT do ensure that we can hear you too!

Submission details


What is the deadline?

October 31, 2014 at midnight!


Where do I submit?

Submit your video here!

I have submitted my video, now what?


Will I become famous by making this video?

We wish it were that easy! This video will NOT be published, publicized or propagated by us.


Who owns the content of the video?

The video and its content belongs solely to you, the person who made it. TEDxLausanne is not responsible for the content.

If you want the video private, upload with settings where only those with the link can see the video.

The video will be viewed exclusively by members of the TEDxLausanne 2015 organizing committee and speaker selection team.


What happens if I am selected?

Congrats!!! A member of our team will be in touch with you to help guide you in preparation of your talk.

Please be aware that a certain time commitment and investment is required on your part to prepare your final talk so it is up to the high standards of a TEDxLausanne event. Expect to meet with us in person to prepare and rehearse your talk. Even the best speakers need to practice to give an excellent talk!


What are the criteria on which my video will be evaluated?

We assess the videos based on the uniqueness, quality, and depth of the ideas presented, the presence of the speaker, and the relevance to our theme.

Again, the quality of the video is less important than the content at this stage!!
